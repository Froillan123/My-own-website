    Coffee

    Coffee drinkers all across the world are probably not thinking about the health benefits or risks of their favorite morning coffee. Nonetheless, this beverage has a lengthy history of controversy. The World Health Organization added coffee to its list of probable carcinogens in 1991. By 2016, it had been exonerated, as study discovered that the beverage was not related with an increased risk of cancer; on the contrary, there was a lower risk of some malignancies among people who drank coffee on a regular basis, after smoking history was correctly accounted for.
